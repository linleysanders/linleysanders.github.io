About Me

I'm a student at Columbia University's Graduate School of Journalism in the Lede Program for data journalism. If you click around, you'll find a few projects I've been building to expand my knowledge of Python, R, PostgreSQL, interactive graphics, web scraping, data cleaning, and basic statistical analysis.

By trade, I'm a journalist who decided coding could make me a better reporter. I've worked for The Associated Press, Newsweek and The Kansas City Star. My passion for data-driven news and women's political interests carried me from Kansas to Iowa to New York City. I'm currently a contributing news reporter for TeenVogue.com, where I've written over 160 articles to help women engage with daily news, politics, and health.
